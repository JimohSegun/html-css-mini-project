About Chicken Flavour Republic

Chicken Flavour Republic is a Nigerian fast-food chain and franchise that specializes in chicken recipes, especially fried chicken.It was founded by Adekemi Moses, and the first restaurant was established in Mushin, Lagos in 2008. The company is arguably Nigeria's largest chicken restaurant chain with over 40 outlets in Lagos and over 200 outlets nationwide. Chicken Flavour Republic has also expanded services to other West African countries including Ghana and Togo.
